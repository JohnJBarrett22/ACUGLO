About AcuGlo

AcuGlo offers wellness solutions tailored to a wide range of conditions which include but are not limited to musculoskeletal pain, infertility, allergies, autoimmune conditions, cancer care, anxiety, cognitive function and more. The mission of AcuGlo is to make cognizant of the options in which care is multifaceted by a custom variation of modalities and treatments to optimize your unique well-being. Just as you are your own unique embodiment, so should your treatment to optimal health.

Our Approach

We are a family-owned and operated acupuncture clinic, where each member of our team brings their own specialties to the office. Our diverse background enables us to handle any illness or injury with ease - we're quite unique that way.

We strive for excellence in all areas by constantly researching and applying new information available on the services we offer, as well as new science behind the ailments you may be experiencing.

We welcome visitors who want more info regarding how they might improve certain conditions outside of the treatment options we offer, so we can help each person live their best life possible.

Our Treatment Solutions

Our service offerings are designed to address the root cause of the problem, not just the symptoms. Our practice addresses everything from arthritis, disease, stress, depression, headaches, physical pain (shoulder pain, neck pain, etc.), insomnia, fatigue and other issues. Through traditional Chinese medicine (TCM), our modalities are strong enough to stand on their own, however can also be for an integrative medicine approach with Chinese and Western medicine.

Acupuncture

The insertion of tiny needles into the skin at strategic points on your body.

Scalp Acupuncture

Needles are used to stimulate the brain cells that are tied to the impaired functions.

Facial Cosmetic Acupuncture

The use of needles with a focus more geared toward beauty than functionality.

Microneedling

Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure that pricks the skin with tiny needles.

Nano Needling

Nano Needling is a non-invasive skin treatment that boasts a multitude of benefits.

Cupping

Vacuum pressure created by heated cups to help improve blood flow within the body.

Herbal Medicine

The use of plants and animals to create remedies that target various ailments.

Moxibustion

Heated dried mugwort coals are placed on specific points to help the condition.

Auricular Acupuncture

Needles inserted into the ear at parts that mirror specific locations on the body.

Electro Lipolysis Acupuncture Program

Electrically stimulated needles placed into acupuncture points to improve ailments.

Light Therapy

Light therapy is a therapy used to treat a variety of mental and skin conditions.

Telehealth

Traditional Chinese medicine conducted through a secure video conference.
