While working as an art director and designer, Corinna was diagnosed with a rare type of lymphoma. This began her journey into healing. Starting with her own body and mind, she began exploring different types of treatments and methods, which led her to begin her studies healing others. Drawing upon her own personal experience with illness, she is able to provide a deeply empathetic approach to clinical care.

Corinna graduated magna cum laude from UCLA. She earned her master’s degree with honors in Traditional Chinese Medicine from Emperor’s College of Oriental Medicine. Her education of Neijing Classical Acupuncture continues with Dr. Edward Neal, MD. She has assisted in oncology and acupuncture research at the Disney Family Cancer Center under Dr. Jung K. Kim, LAc. and Dr. Ora Gordon, MD and continues to work with patients recovering from chemotherapy with Dr. Jung K. Kim, LAc.
