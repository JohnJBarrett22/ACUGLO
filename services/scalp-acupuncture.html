Scalp Acupuncture

What is scalp acupuncture?

Scalp acupuncture, also called head acupuncture is a contemporary acupuncture technique integrating traditional Chinese needling methods with Western medical knowledge of representative areas of the cerebral cortex. According to research, it has been proven to be the most effective technique for treating acute and chronic central nervous system disorders.

Scalp acupuncture is said to be beneficial for conditions such as headaches, migraines, neck pain, and back pain. It is also said to be helpful for conditions such as stroke, paralysis, and cerebral palsy.

There is scientific evidence to support the use of scalp acupuncture for certain conditions. For example, one study found that scalp acupuncture was effective in reducing pain after a stroke.

How long does scalp acupuncture last?

There is no definitive answer to the question of how long scalp acupuncture results last, as each person's response to treatment may be different. However, many people who have undergone this type of treatment report experiencing significant improvements in their symptoms, which can often last for several months or longer. In some cases, the effects of scalp acupuncture may even be permanent.

If you are considering undergoing scalp acupuncture treatment, it is important to consult with us to discuss your specific case and expectations. We will be able to provide you with more personalized information about what you can expect from treatment.

Is scalp acupuncture painful?

So, is scalp acupuncture painful? The short answer is no. While some patients report a slight tingling sensation during the treatment, traditional Chinese scalp acupuncture is generally not painful.

That being said, everyone experiences pain differently. Some people may find traditional Chinese scalp acupuncture to be more uncomfortable than others. If you have concerns about pain, be sure to discuss them with us beforehand. We will be happy to answer any questions you have.

At the end of the day, traditional Chinese scalp acupuncture is a gentle and effective treatment for a variety of conditions. If you are looking for an alternative to traditional medicine, this may be the treatment for you.

What are the benefits of scalp acupuncture?

There is a growing body of evidence to suggest that scalp acupuncture can be an effective treatment for a wide range of conditions, including headaches, stroke, and Parkinson’s disease.

  • Relief from pain
  • Reduced inflammation
  • Improved circulation
  • Simulation of the nervous system
  • Release of endorphins (the body's natural painkillers)
  • Promotion of relaxation

If you are considering scalp acupuncture as a treatment option, it is important to consult with us to discuss how this may benefit you.

What can scalp acupuncture help with?

There is scientific evidence to support the use of scalp acupuncture for the treatment of certain medical conditions, such as headaches, stroke, and chronic pain. Scalp acupuncture has also been shown to be effective in treating mental health conditions, such as anxiety and depression. In traditional Chinese medicine, scalp acupuncture is used to treat a wide range of conditions, including digestive issues, reproductive issues, and neurological disorders.

  • rehabilitation of paralysis due to stroke
  • multiple sclerosis
  • automobile accident
  • Parkinson's disease
  • pain management, especially caused by the central nervous system such as phantom pain,
  • complex regional pain
  • residual limb pain
  • aphasia
  • loss of balance
  • loss of hearing
  • dizziness and vertigo
