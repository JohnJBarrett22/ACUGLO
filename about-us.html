About Us

Jennifer Ryu, DAOM, L.Ac, NCCAOM

Jennifer followed into her father’s footsteps and has adopted the approach of treating the root causes and following the journey to wellness. She is board certified and uses traditional acupuncture, Korean hand acupuncture, auricular acupuncture, herbal medicine and many other techniques and modalities. She has a background working in autoimmune conditions, cancer, fertility, pain, and many other chronic conditions.

In her current enrollment of her Doctorate program, she is furthering her area of interest in fertility to women of and beyond the primal reproductive years. Her goal is to create a simpler understanding or dialogue to bridge the gap between and Eastern and Western medicine.

Veronica Ryu, L.Ac, NCCAOM

Veronica is a board certified acupuncturist and focuses on synergy of the mind and body. With her skills in traditional acupuncture, hand acupuncture, and herbal medicine, she has a strong motivation for women’s health, mental health, and daily functioning of overall wellness. She has a background working with fertility, pain, autoimmune conditions, cancer, and many other chronic conditions.

She vehemently pursues her passion in creating a deeper understanding of that strange link between the human mind and energy.
