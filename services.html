Cupping

Fire cupping, also known as cupping therapy, is an alternative, non-invasive treatment technique that places glass, bamboo, or plastic jars on the skin to enhance circulation, relieve pain, and extract toxins from the body. This unique therapy is often recommended to patients who suffer from muscular pain, joint pain, skin problems, and/or respiratory disorders.

  1. Pain reduction and changes in biomechanical properties of the skin could be explained by “Pain-Gate Theory”, “Diffuse Noxious Inhibitory Controls” and “Reflex zone theory”.
  2. Muscle relaxation, changes in local tissue structures and increase in blood circulation explained by “Nitric Oxide theory”.
  3. Immunological effects and hormonal adjustments by “Activation of immune system theory”.
  4. Releasing of toxins and removal of wastes and heavy metals might be explained by “Blood Detoxification Theory”
  • Fire cupping was beneficial in the treatment of acne, herpes zoster, and chronic pain. -the Journal of Traditional and Complementary Medicine.
  • Fire cupping was a safe and effective treatment modality for several diseases and conditions, including acne, facial paralysis, and cervical spondylosis.
  • cupping can be used to mitigate arthritis, fibromyelgia, high blood pressure, migraine, anxiety, depression, allergies, asthma, varicose vein, and many more -The British Cupping Society

Fire cupping is deemed a safe procedure, but side effects can still arise. Patients may experience mild discomfort, bruising, burns, and in rare instances, mild infections during and after the cupping therapy. Moxibustion

Moxibustion is a traditional medical treatment originating in China. It involves using the heat of burning moxa to stimulate acupoints. It is considered safe and effective and is widely used throughout the world.

Auricular Acupuncture

What is auricular acupuncture?

Auricular acupuncture is a diagnostic and treatment system based on normalizing the body's dysfunction through stimulation of definite points on the ear. It can be used for a wide range of indications.

  • Various painful diseases
  • Infectious diseases
  • Functional disorders (irregular heartbeat, neurasthenia, hysteria, irregular menstruation, sexual dysfunction, irritable bowl syndrome)
  • Allergic diseases
  • Hormonal and endocrine imbalances
  • Epidemic diseases
  • Chronic diseases (back pain, leg pain, frozen shoulder, indigestion, numbness of extremities)
  • Wellness care
  • Addiction
